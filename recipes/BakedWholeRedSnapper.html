The Baked Whole Red Snapper

This baked red snapper is cooked with aromatics and Cajun spices that will have your taste buds dancing. Red snapper is a mild and lean fish with a firm texture.

Baked Whole Red Snapper Ingredients

  • 1 whole red snapper (about 2 to 3 pounds), cleaned and scaled
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 450 degrees F (230 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking sheet or line it with parchment paper.

  2. Step 2

    3. Rinse the red snapper under cold water and pat it dry with paper towels. Make 3 to 4 diagonal slashes on each side of the fish. Rub the fish inside and out with olive oil, garlic, paprika, Cajun seasoning, salt, and black pepper. Stuff the cavity with lemon slices.

  3. Step 3

    4. Place the prepared red snapper on the baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork.

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove the fish from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh parsley and additional lemon slices if desired. Serve hot.
