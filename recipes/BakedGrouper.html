The Baked Grouper

This baked grouper recipe is a delightful way to enjoy a mild and flaky fish. With a simple seasoning of lemon, garlic, and herbs, this dish is both flavorful and easy to prepare.

Baked Grouper Ingredients

  • 4 grouper fillets (about 6 ounces each)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 lemon, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme or oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Lightly grease a baking dish with olive oil.

  2. Step 2

    3. Rinse the grouper fillets under cold water and pat them dry with paper towels. Place the fillets in the prepared baking dish.

  3. Step 3

    4. Drizzle olive oil over the fillets and sprinkle with minced garlic, dried thyme or oregano, salt, and pepper. Arrange lemon slices on top of the fillets.

  4. Step 4

    5. Bake in the preheated oven for 15 to 20 minutes, or until the fish is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Garnish with fresh parsley if desired before serving.
